# Lasagna - Grandma's Recipe.

It's non-traditional and it's the best!
<!-- more -->
{{ recipe_header() }}

**Ingredients**

- 1 lb ground beef          
- 1 lb italian sausage      
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce (approximate) 
- 1/2 diced onion (optional)
- 1 box lasagna noodles 
- 1 package frozen spinach  
- 2 lbs Mozzerella or Monterey Jack Cheese (Shredded)   
- 2 cans cream of mushroom soup 
- 3/4 can milk              
- 1 small can tomato sauce  
- Oregano - to taste        
- Salt - to taste           
- Parmesan cheese - to taste   
- Garlic Salt - to taste    
- Dried Parsley - taste     

**Step-by-Step Instructions**

- Brown the ground beef and sausage together. If adding onion, cook it with the meat. Add 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (this is approximate and may adjust to your taste).
- Cook spinach according to directions and add to the meat mixture once the meat is cooked through
- Cook 1 box of lasagna noodles and drain; pat dry with paper towels
- Heat 2 cans cream of mushroom soup with 3/4 can of milk. It does not need to be hot, just warm enough to make it smooth and creamy.
- In a greased lasagna pan, add one layer of noodles, then hald the meat mixture and half the cheese. Then repeat one more time.
- Slowly pour the soup over the top of the lasagna. Then to make it pretty, swirl the tomato sauce on the top.
- Add parmesan cheese, garlic salt, and parley flakes (done to your taste)
- Cover the pan with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes. It will be bubbly when done.
